# HIIT Timer App — Default Preset Values

| Level | Warm-up   | Work speed | Rest speed | Cool-down | Work interval | Rest interval | Session duration | Rounds |
|-------|-----------|------------|------------|-----------|---------------|---------------|------------------|--------|
| 1     | 5 km/h    | 8 km/h     | 5 km/h     | 4.5 km/h  | 20 sec        | 40 sec        | ~25 min          | 14     |
| 2     | 6 km/h    | 11 km/h    | 6 km/h     | 5.5 km/h  | 30 sec        | 30 sec        | ~26 min          | 18     |
| 3     | 7 km/h    | 14 km/h    | 7 km/h     | 6.0 km/h  | 45 sec        | 15 sec        | ~32 min          | 22     |
| 4     | 8 km/h    | 17 km/h    | 8 km/h     | 6.5 km/h  | 50 sec        | 10 sec        | ~35 min          | 25     |
| 5     | 9 km/h    | 20 km/h    | 9 km/h     | 7.0 km/h  | 55 sec        | 10 sec        | ~37 min          | 25     |
| 6     | 10 km/h   | 23 km/h    | 10 km/h    | 7.5 km/h  | 60 sec        | 5 sec         | ~39 min          | 27     |

*Session duration = warmup + work/rest intervals + cooldown.*

## Key Principles

- **Warm-up/cool-down:** 3 min at level 1–2, 5 min at levels 3–6
- **Work:rest ratio** progresses from 1:2 (level 1) → 1:1 (level 2) → 3:1 (level 3) → 5:1 (level 4) → 5.5:1 (level 5) → 12:1 (level 6)
- **Speeds are highly individual** — these are sensible defaults, user should be able to edit freely
- **Levels 4–6 are extrapolated** from the easy/medium/hard progression (+3 km/h work speed per level, +1 km/h warmup/rest, diminishing rest intervals)
