# HIIT Timer App — Default Preset Values

| Level  | Warm-up  | Work speed | Rest speed | Cool-down | Work interval | Rest interval | Session duration | Rounds |
|--------|----------|------------|------------|-----------|---------------|---------------|------------------|--------|
| Easy   | 5 km/h   | 8 km/h     | 5 km/h     | 4.5 km/h  | 20 sec        | 40 sec        | 12 min           | ~14    |
| Medium | 6 km/h   | 11 km/h    | 6 km/h     | 5.5 km/h  | 30 sec        | 30 sec        | 18 min           | 18     |
| Hard   | 7 km/h   | 14 km/h    | 7 km/h     | 6 km/h    | 45 sec        | 15 sec        | 22 min           | ~22    |

## Key Principles

- **Warm-up/cool-down:** 3–5 min, scaling slightly with fitness level
- **Work:rest ratio** progresses from 1:2 (easy) → 1:1 (medium) → 3:1 (hard)
- **Total session time** including warm-up/cool-down lands at 18–35 minutes
- **Speeds are highly individual** — these are sensible defaults, user should be able to edit freely
- **Hard sessions cap at ~25 min;** beyond that it stops being true HIIT
